UZUMAKI NARUTO

Hokage Dattebayo

Background and Abilities

  • Became junchuriki of nine tails on day of his birth.
  • Villagers considered him as incarnation of evil and was always neglected.
  • Naruto worked hard to gain the villager's acknowledgement all the while chasing his dream to become Hokage.
  • Being Uzumaki and a junchuriki has large reserves of chakra.
  • Most used techeniques are: Rasengan, Shadow Clone Jutsu, RasenShuriken,Tailed Beast Transformation, Senjutsu and Sexy Jutsu
  • Has 11 nature release with most affinity to Wind release and 3 kekkei genkai Lava Release, Magnet Release and Boil Release.
